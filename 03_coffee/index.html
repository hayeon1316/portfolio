Our goals

Our mission is to make good coffee accessible and exciting, simple to understand and appreciate

  • FIRST
    HAVING
    A POSITIVE IMPACT

    We strive to contribute to, and have a positive impact on, the communities we’re connected to through Coffee Roastery

  • SECOND
    TRACEABILITY AND
    TRANSPARENCY

    Transparency is integral to our business at every level. We have fostered a culture that encourages direct, open and transparent communication

  • THIRD
    EXCEPTIONAL
    QUALITY

    We seek out and buy the best coffees from producers who share our values and are committed to quality

  • FOURTH
    REDUCING OUR
    ENVIRONMENTAL IMPACT

    We are constantly pursuing ways to reduce our impact on the environment.
